Jonkioping is a city located in South Sweden.

What can you do when you visit the city?

There are a few things that the city is famous for. Did you know that the safety match was invented in Jonkioping? Well now you know. If you happen to be in the city you should visit the Matchstik Museum. There you will find out about the invention that turned the city into a match-producing capital from 1845 to 1970.

You are interested in Jonkioping's history and culture? If yes, another place that you should visit is the Habo Church. It is has the demension of a cathedral but is made comletely from wood. Also on the inside the entire interior is covored with paintings ilustrating Marthin Luther's Catechesis.

If you just want to enjoy the beautiful nature of Jonkioping you should visit the Vatterstrand and have a walk around the lake. Another nice place that you can have a fika in is the Roselunds Rosarium which is located near the Vatterstrand.
